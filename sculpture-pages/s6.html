The Vaccinator

2021

H35cm x W30cm x D25cm

Electrical parts and miscellaneous components

$1,200

During the civil war, the vaccinator was an important device designed to spray soldiers with vaccinations on the battlefield to protect them from the scourge of disease. During the Battle of Berezat, the vaccinator was accidentally fueled with Love Potion No 9. Needless to say that the battle involved way less shooting and much more hugging.
