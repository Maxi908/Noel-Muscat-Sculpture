About the exhibition

Metal Morphix brings together the exciting work of Andre Sardone and Noel Muscat. These longtime friends inspire each other to push the boundaries of their practice as they explore wasy to morph materials into highly expressive sculpture.

's amazing nonsensical objects are compiled of recognisable and juxtaposed machinery, industrial parts, laboratory and photographic equipment. His works sits inquisitively alongide Andre's as he showcases his recent explorations into innovative techniques for joining, machining and milling raw metals, exploring forms inspired by natural seed structures that protect, nurture, and ensure the survival of plant species. Together they have created a collection that sparks true curiosity and wonder.
